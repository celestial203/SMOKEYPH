About Smokey Peeks

Where Chicago-style BBQ meets the island spirit of Cebu.

Our Story

Smokey Peeks began with a simple idea — bring something truly different to Cebu.

Growing up Filipino, hospitality, family gatherings, and great food were always part of life. But when Chicago barbecue entered the picture through my partner from Chicago, we knew we had something special. The deep smoke, bold spices, slow cooking, and rich traditions of American BBQ were unlike anything most people experience in the islands.

So we decided to bring the two worlds together. Smokey Peeks was created to blend Chicago smokehouse tradition with Cebu warmth and island hospitality. Every recipe, every detail, and every plate that leaves our kitchen represents that story. Slow-smoked meats, crafted flavors, great drinks, music, and an atmosphere where people can relax, celebrate, and enjoy the moment.

From our first location at One Pavilion Mall to our seaside destination at IL Corso, Smokey Peeks continues to grow because of the amazing community around us.

We're proud to bring something new to Cebu — a place where Chicago flavor meets island life.
